It’s a camera control and presentation app.

This is a web-based Progressive Web App (PWA) that runs in the Chrome browser. It lets you view, control, and manipulate the live feed from a document camera, making it ideal for classrooms, remote learning, product demos, tutorials, and virtual presentations.

This app is a web-based application designed for ChromeOS and runs directly in the Chrome browser. To use this app in a Zoom, Meet, or Teams call, simply share the browser window or the tab where the app is running.

No internet connection is required to use the app. All features—including live view, annotations, and snapshots—work offline. If you'd like to save screenshots to Google Drive, you'll need to sign in with your Google account.

ChromeOS devices and UVC-compliant document cameras

This app is designed for ChromeOS and runs directly in the Chrome browser. It supports all UVC-compliant document cameras, meaning plug-and-play compatibility with most modern USB cameras.

Simple setup to get started

Open the app in your Chrome browser and connect your UVC-compliant document camera via USB. Once connected, you’ll be able to present your camera feed, adjust settings such as focus, zoom, and exposure, and utilize other image manipulation tools in real-time.
You can also share this live feed during video calls simply by sharing your browser tab or window.
